Essential Tips

Local wisdom to make your stay effortless and unforgettable

Best Time to Visit

Year-round destination with trade winds keeping temperatures comfortable. Avoid hurricane season (June-November) for most predictable weather.

Currency & Payments

Dutch Antillean Guilder (ANG) is local currency, but USD is widely accepted. Credit cards work at most establishments.

Languages

Dutch and Papiamentu are official, but English and Spanish are widely spoken. Don't hesitate to communicate!

Getting Around

Rental car included with your villa stay! Driving is on the right side, roads are well-maintained, and GPS works reliably.

What to Pack

Light, breathable clothing, strong sunscreen (reef-safe), comfortable sandals, and swimwear for multiple beach days.

Local Etiquette

Friendly greetings are important. "Bon dia" (good day) goes a long way. Tipping 10-15% is appreciated but not mandatory.
