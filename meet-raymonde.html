The Island Insiders: Meet Raymonde "The Operations Master", The Guest Experience Visionary

Words by Tommy Coconut | September 2025

In the world of luxury hospitality, precision isn't just preferred—it's essential. Meet Raymonde, the Guest Experience Manager whose meticulous nature and unwavering high standards have transformed the Tommy Coconut experience into something truly extraordinary. Known for her sharp eye for detail coupled with a warm, welcoming personality, Raymonde creates an atmosphere of perfection in every property while completing her final year of hospitality school.

Raymonde possesses that rare combination of educational excellence and practical mastery that defines true hospitality professionals. She doesn't just manage guest experiences—she crafts them with the precision of an artist and the heart of someone who genuinely cares. Her approach is rooted in what Curaçaoans call atenshon perfekto – the understanding that extraordinary experiences are built on flawless execution of every single detail.

The Creative Force Behind the Brand

Beyond guest experience management, Raymonde serves as the creative visionary behind Tommy Coconut's entire digital presence. She's the artistic mind crafting the family's social media strategy, designing their website, and producing videos that showcase the breathtaking beauty of Curaçao to the world. Her deep appreciation for aesthetics transforms every piece of content into a window into the bida bunita that awaits guests.

Her goal isn't just to maintain Tommy Coconut's reputation – it's to continuously elevate the brand and share the magic of island life with people around the globe. Through her lens, potential guests don't just see vacation rentals; they see possibilities, adventures, and the promise of memories that will last a lifetime.

This creative work isn't separate from her guest experience role – it's perfectly integrated. Raymonde understands that the digital experience is the first touchpoint for most guests, and she ensures that the beauty and warmth they encounter online is seamlessly continued when they arrive on the island.

Captain of Caribbean Adventures

But perhaps the most magical aspect of Raymonde's role is one that surprises many guests: she's also the captain of the boat used for Tommy Coconut's legendary sunset cruises. Picture this—after a day of ensuring every detail of your land-based experience is perfect, the same person who crafted your stay now guides you across the stunning Caribbean waters as the sun paints the sky in impossible colors.

As captain, Raymonde brings the same meticulous attention to detail and commitment to excellence that defines her guest experience work. Her expert guidance transforms a simple boat ride into an adventure filled with local insights, perfect photo opportunities, and moments of pure Caribbean magi that guests remember for years.

This unique combination of roles—from digital creative to sunset cruise captain—perfectly embodies what makes the Tommy Coconut experience so special. It's not just about professional service; it's about genuine, multifaceted relationships where team members share their diverse talents and passions with guests.

Living the Happy Island Life

Beyond her professional accomplishments, Raymonde has also pursued her passion for modeling, collaborating with renowned brands while maintaining her commitment to the family business. This additional experience enriches her contributions to Tommy Coconut's marketing efforts, bringing a professional model's understanding of visual storytelling to every piece of content she creates.

Raymonde embodies the spirit of "Living the Happy Island life" – not just as a motto, but as a genuine way of being that she and her family embrace wholeheartedly. This authenticity shines through in everything she does, from the way she frames a sunset photo to the warmth in her voice as she welcomes guests aboard for their cruise.

The Future Leader

As Raymonde finishes her hospitality thesis and prepares to graduate, her education combined with years of practical experience positions her as a future leader who will carry on the legacy of exceptional service. Her aspiration isn't just to maintain Tommy Coconut's already high standards—it's to elevate them even further while staying true to the family values that make the experience so special.

Her deep bond with her family, both in personal life and business endeavors, creates a foundation for continued excellence. Her passion for creating unforgettable experiences aligns perfectly with the Tommy Coconut mission, ensuring that the next generation of leadership will honor the past while innovating for the future.

For guests, Raymonde represents the perfect fusion of professional expertise and genuine Caribbean warmth. Whether she's ensuring your villa is flawlessly prepared, sharing the beauty of Curaçao through her creative content, or guiding you across crystal-clear waters as the sun sets, her unwavering commitment to excellence and her dedication to guest satisfaction make every moment inolvida.
