15 Best Beaches in Curaçao

From the pristine shores of Klein Curaçao to hidden local gems, discover the beaches that make our island a true Caribbean paradise. Each one tells its own story.

After years of exploring every corner of our island, we've curated this list of Curaçao's most spectacular beaches. Whether you're seeking adventure, tranquility, or the perfect snorkeling spot, these beaches offer something for every soul seeking the dushi life.

1

Klein Curaçao

An uninhabited island paradise with crystal-clear waters and pristine white sand. The boat ride alone is an adventure, and the snorkeling here is unmatched.

Boat Trip Required Best Snorkeling Day Trip
2

Cas Abao Beach

A perfect family beach with facilities, shade, and calm waters. The underwater life here is spectacular, making it ideal for beginners learning to snorkel.

Family Friendly Full Facilities Great Snorkeling
3

Playa Kenepa (Grote Knip)

Instagram-famous for its turquoise waters framed by dramatic cliffs. Perfect for cliff jumping (for the brave) and swimming in impossibly blue water.

Cliff Jumping Turquoise Waters Photography
4

Playa Porto Mari

Home to resident wild pigs that roam the beach! Double reef system makes it perfect for all snorkeling levels. Beach beds and restaurant on site.

Wild Pigs Double Reef Restaurant
5

Jan Thiel Beach

The social beach with beach clubs, restaurants, and water sports. Perfect for those who like their beach days with a side of luxury and cocktails.

Beach Clubs Water Sports Nightlife
6

Playa Lagun

A small cove beach perfect for snorkeling and diving. Sea turtles are regular visitors here. The intimate setting makes it feel like your private paradise.

Sea Turtles Diving Spot Intimate Cove
7

Playa Piskado (Playa Grandi)

The turtle beach! Local fishermen clean their catch here, attracting sea turtles daily. Swimming with these gentle giants is an unforgettable experience.

Guaranteed Turtles Local Vibes Free Entry
8

Mambo Beach

The party beach with shops, restaurants, and regular events. Close to Willemstad, it's perfect for combining beach time with city exploration.

Shopping Live Music City Close
9

Kleine Knip (Playa Kenepa Chiki)

The smaller sister of Grote Knip but equally beautiful. More intimate and often less crowded, with the same stunning turquoise waters.

Less Crowded Intimate Crystal Waters
10

Blue Bay Beach

Golf course adjacent beach with excellent facilities. The artificial reef attracts colorful fish, making it great for families with young snorkelers.

Golf Resort Kids Club Safe Swimming
11

Director's Bay

A hidden gem for advanced divers and snorkelers. The current can be strong, but the underwater scenery and marine life are worth it for experienced swimmers.

Advanced Diving Hidden Gem Marine Life
12

Kokomo Beach

A locals' favorite with a laid-back vibe. Small but perfectly formed, with a beach bar serving cold beers and fresh seafood. Dog-friendly too!

Local Favorite Dog Friendly Beach Bar
13

Santa Barbara Beach

Long stretch of white sand beach perfect for walking. Part of a resort but public access available. Great for watching planes land at the nearby airport.

Long Beach Walks Plane Spotting Resort Access
14

Boca Sami

A secluded beach requiring a short hike. The effort is rewarded with privacy and untouched natural beauty. Bring everything you need - there are no facilities.

Secluded Short Hike Natural Beauty
15

Playa Kalki (Alice in Wonderland)

Named after the famous dive site, this beach offers world-class diving and snorkeling. The underwater mushroom coral formations are truly magical.

World-Class Diving Mushroom Corals Dive Shop

Insider Beach Tips from the Tommy Coconut Family

Best Times to Visit

Early morning (before 10 AM) or late afternoon (after 3 PM) for most beaches. You'll avoid crowds and harsh sun. Klein Curaçao trips typically leave at 7 AM and return by 5 PM.

Beach Fees

Most beaches charge a small entrance fee (usually $3-6 USD per car). Cas Abao and Porto Mari have higher fees but include facilities. Playa Piskado and public beaches like Kenepa are free.

Snorkeling Gear

Bring your own or rent at most beaches for $10-15. Pro tip: Buy a set at a local shop for about $25 - you'll use it every day! Don't forget reef-safe sunscreen.

Getting There

Your Tommy Coconut Jeep is perfect for beach hopping! West coast beaches (Cas Abao, Porto Mari, Kenepa) make a great day trip. East side beaches (Jan Thiel, Mambo) are closer to Willemstad.
