Quick Answers

Does the EV-SUV fit 6 people plus luggage?

Yes. It's a full-size 7-passenger SUV with third-row seating and ample cargo space for families with luggage and beach gear.

What about beach loungers at Jan Thiel Beach?

The Ultimate Beach Kit includes portable chairs, umbrellas, and coolers for any beach. Jan Thiel Beach Club loungers can be reserved separately - we'll handle it for you.

Can I book fewer than 7 nights?

Sometimes. Message us with your dates and we'll check availability for shorter stays.

Is electricity really included?

Yes, absolutely. Electricity for the villa and EV charging is included in your all-inclusive package. Zero hidden fees means zero hidden fees.

What's NOT included?

Only your flights, alcohol beyond welcome dinner, additional restaurant meals, and optional activities beyond what's included. Everything else is covered.

What's your cancellation policy?

Flexible cancellation up to 30 days before arrival for full refund. Within 30 days, deposits are non-refundable but can be applied to future dates within 12 months.
