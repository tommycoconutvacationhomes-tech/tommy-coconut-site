The Island Insiders: Meet Juan Carlos "MacGyver", The Master Problem Solver

Words by Tommy Coconut | September 2025

There's a reason Juan Carlos earned the nickname "MacGyver" – and it's exactly what you'd expect. Known affectionately as the family's maintenance wizard, there's no challenge too big or too small for him to tackle. From fixing a leaky roof to maintaining the family's fleet of vintage jeeps, Juan Carlos truly can do it all. His legendary mantra, "Ain't a problem Carlos can't fix - he can do it in the mix," reflects his unwavering dedication to ensuring that everything runs smoothly in the family's Private Resort properties.

Juan Carlos possesses that rare gift of improvisation that makes him invaluable to the Tommy Coconut operation. Just like the iconic TV character whose nickname he proudly bears, he can find creative solutions to any maintenance issue, often using everyday items to get the job done. His resourcefulness has earned him the trust and admiration of the entire family, and his magia di repara has become legendary among guests who witness his work in action.

The Three-Times Champion

What truly sets Juan Carlos apart is his incredible ability to fix anything – and we mean anything – in just three attempts. This isn't just family folklore; it's a proven track record that has saved countless guest experiences. Whether it's a complex electrical issue, a mechanical breakdown, or a completely unique problem no one has seen before, Juan Carlos approaches each challenge with the confidence that comes from knowing he can solve it within three tries.

His maestria di tres biaha has become legendary within the Tommy Coconut operation. This methodical approach isn't just about confidence – it's about understanding that the first attempt teaches you what doesn't work, the second attempt gets you closer to the solution, and the third attempt seals the deal. It's problem-solving as both art and science.

The Tommy Coconut Cocktail Specialist

Beyond his maintenance mastery, Juan Carlos has taken charge of something equally important to the guest experience: coconut delivery for the famous Tommy Coconut Cocktail. This beloved treat among the family's guests requires the freshest, most perfect coconuts, and Juan Carlos's attention to detail ensures that each cocktail is prepared with ingredients that meet his exacting standards.

His responsibility for the Tommy Coconut Cocktail program demonstrates how his meticulous nature extends beyond mechanical problems to every aspect of the guest experience. Whether he's sourcing the perfect coconuts, ensuring proper storage, or coordinating with the team for cocktail preparation, Juan Carlos brings the same dedikas perfekto to this delicious responsibility as he does to fixing air conditioners.

The Essential Family Asset

The Tommy Coconut family is deeply grateful to have Juan Carlos as their maintenance guru. His ability to fix, repair, and improve anything and everything has become an integral part of their success. From the smallest villa details to major infrastructure challenges, Juan Carlos approaches every task with the same unwavering dedication and creative problem-solving that defines his legendary reputation.

His expertise extends far beyond basic repairs – he's constantly innovating and improving systems to enhance the guest experience while making operations more efficient for the family. His preventive maintenance approach means problems are solved before guests ever know they existed, and his rapid response to unexpected issues ensures that vacation plans are never disrupted.

For guests, Juan Carlos represents the ultimate peace of mind. They may never see him working behind the scenes, but they benefit from his expertise in every perfectly functioning appliance, every smoothly operating system, and every moment when their vacation unfolds exactly as planned. His presence in the Tommy Coconut operation means that technical problems simply don't exist in the guest experience.

Whether he's maintaining the family's fleet of vintage jeeps, ensuring the villas are in perfect condition, or sourcing the freshest coconuts for the signature cocktail, Juan Carlos embodies the principle that true luxury is when everything works so perfectly that guests never have to think about the complexity behind their comfort. His mantra says it all: "Ain't a problem Carlos can't fix – he can do it in the mix," and in three tries or less, that garantia has never been broken.
