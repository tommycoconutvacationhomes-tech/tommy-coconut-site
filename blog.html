Essential Curaçao Tips

Insider knowledge from the Tommy Coconut family

Getting Around

Rent a car or choose a Tommy Coconut villa with included vehicle. Taxis are expensive, and having wheels gives you freedom to explore hidden beaches.

Best Time to Visit

Year-round paradise! Avoid September-November for hurricanes. December-April is peak season. May-August offers great weather with fewer crowds.

Money Matters

USD widely accepted. Local currency is Florin (ANG). Credit cards work most places. Always tip 10-15% at restaurants - it's appreciated!

Local Language

Dutch and Papiamentu are official, but English is widely spoken. Learn "Dushi" and "Bon Bini" - locals love it! Don't forget to say "Masha Danki" when they help you!

Beach Essentials

Bring reef-safe sunscreen (protect our coral!), water shoes for rocky entries, and snorkel gear. Many beaches have great snorkeling right off the shore.

Stay Connected

All our Private Resorts include high-speed WiFi. International roaming can be expensive, so consider local SIM cards for longer stays.
