Booking & Payments

We believe in fair, transparent policies that protect both you and us:

Payment Policy:

  • Reservation deposit: 25% due at booking to secure your dates
  • Final payment: Remaining balance due 60 days before arrival
  • Accepted methods: Credit cards, bank transfers, and secure online payments

Cancellation Policy:

  • 60+ days before arrival: Full refund minus processing fees
  • 30-59 days before arrival: 50% refund
  • Less than 30 days: No refund, but we'll work with you to reschedule if possible

We understand that life happens. If you need to cancel due to unforeseen circumstances, please contact us immediately. We're always willing to work with you to find the best solution.
