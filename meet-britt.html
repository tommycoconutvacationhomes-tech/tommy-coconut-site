The Island Insiders: Meet Britt "The Anchor", The Foundation of Love

Words by Tommy Coconut | September 2025

Some people enter your life and immediately make everything feel warmer, more welcoming, and infinitely more joyful. For the Tommy Coconut family, that person is Britt. Known for her warm and sweet personality, she's not just Boy's girlfriend – she's become an integral part of the extended Tommy Coconut family, bringing her own special brand of positivity and care to every guest experience.

Britt's journey from the Netherlands to Curaçao is a beautiful testament to her adventurous spirit and love for the island. Her transition into the family business showcases the strong bonds that exist among all the family members, and her seamless integration creates the warm, welcoming atmosphere that guests experience from the moment they arrive. Her enthusiasm for the Caribbean way of life adds authentic alegria to the family's commitment to "Living the Happy Island life."

The Heart of Financial Excellence

As the family's dedicated bookkeeper, Britt brings the same warmth and attention to detail to financial management that she brings to guest relations. She ensures that the financial aspects of the Tommy Coconut business are always in perfect order, managing budgets, tracking expenses, and handling financial records with meticulous care. Her role extends far beyond numbers—she understands that sound financial management is what allows the family to continue providing exceptional experiences.

Though she may be early in her career within the family business, Britt's dedication and eagerness to learn have made her an invaluable team member. Her willingness to take on responsibilities and support the family's mission demonstrates her commitment to their shared success.

Britt's positive outlook on life and natural ability to connect with both guests and family members make her a source of optimism and cheerfulness within the Tommy Coconut operation. Her presence adds to the already delightful atmosphere that defines their hospitalidad approach.

Boy's Right Hand

As Boy's assistant in welcoming and checking in guests, Britt plays a crucial role in creating those magical first impressions that set the tone for entire vacations. Her supportive partnership with Boy allows him to shine as the charismatic host while ensuring that every logistical detail is handled with care and precision.

Together, they form a dynamic duo that ensures guests feel immediately welcomed and that their needs are met from the moment they arrive. Britt's natural warmth complements Boy's exuberant energy, creating a balanced welcome experience that makes every guest feel like they're arriving at a family reunion rather than a vacation rental.

Her role alongside Boy also provides her with invaluable firsthand experience in hospitality and entrepreneurship. This hands-on learning aligns perfectly with her aspirations to contribute even more to the family's success as she continues to grow within the business. Her ambicion to expand her role demonstrates her commitment to the Tommy Coconut mission.

A Love Story of Adventure

Britt's journey to Curaçao represents more than just a career move—it's a beautiful love story that exemplifies the adventurous spirit and passion for life that defines the Tommy Coconut experience. Her relationship with Boy and her embrace of island culture demonstrate that life at Tommy Coconut is truly about creating beautiful memories and meaningful connections.

Her enthusiastic adoption of the Caribbean way of life adds an extra layer of authenticity to the family's commitment to "Living the Happy Island life." Guests can see and feel this genuine love for the island in every interaction, knowing that Britt's recommendations and care come from a place of real experience and genuine pasion for the destination.

Building the Future Together

Britt's role as both bookkeeper and Boy's assistant reflects her commitment to supporting the family business from multiple angles. Her combination of financial responsibility and front-line guest service gives her a unique perspective on what it takes to create exceptional experiences while maintaining a sustainable business.

Her positivity, warmth, and dedication to ensuring both the financial health and guest satisfaction of the Tommy Coconut operation make her an invaluable member of the team. As she continues to grow within the business, her multi-faceted skills and genuine care for both numbers and people position her as a future leader who understands all aspects of successful hospitality.

For guests, Britt represents the perfect blend of professional competence and personal warmth. Whether she's handling the behind-the-scenes financial details that keep everything running smoothly or welcoming you with Boy's infectious energy, her commitment to excellence and genuine care for guest satisfaction ensures that every stay is marked by both seamless logistics and heartfelt atencion.
