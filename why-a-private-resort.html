Good to know

We're not all-inclusive (by design)

Tommy Coconut is a Private Resort—not an all-inclusive resort. That means you won't find buffets, wristbands, or pre-programmed activities. Instead, you get:

  • A luxury home with a private pool
  • Your own EV-SUV to explore the island
  • Private boat day, Friday cruise, and flamingo hike included
  • Reservations at the best local restaurants (you pay for meals)
  • Reserved access to 4 beach clubs
  • WhatsApp Concierge to customize your week

You have the freedom to explore, dine where you want, and experience Curaçao on your terms—without the limitations of a traditional all-inclusive package.

Private Resort Package includes

  • Luxury accommodation: Private villa or apartment with pool
  • EV-SUV: Tesla Model Y or BYD Atto 3 included (free charging at home)
  • Airport transfers: Jeremiah meets you at arrivals
  • Welcome provisions: Fridge stocked, lights on, home ready
  • 3 signature experiences: Private boat day, Friday Family & Friends cruise, private flamingo hike
  • Beach club access: Reserved windows & parking at Zanzibar, Papagayo, Koko's, Zest
  • Restaurant reservations: Pre-intro at top local hotspots (meals not included)
  • WhatsApp Concierge: 3-option system (Good / Great / Tommy's Choice)
  • Family gear: Cribs, highchairs, snorkels, SUP/kayak, beach chairs, cooler, beach safe
  • Mid-stay housekeeping: Fresh linens, towels, and cleaning
  • No hidden fees: Tips, water, electricity, cleaning, and laundry included

Clarity first: what's included

We believe in complete transparency. Your nightly rate includes everything listed above. What you'll pay for separately:

  • Meals at restaurants (we book, you enjoy)
  • Groceries and alcohol
  • Optional add-ons (private chef, spa services, extra boat trips)

No resort fees. No surprise surcharges. No security deposits. Just clarity.
