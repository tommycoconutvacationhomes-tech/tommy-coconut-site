The Complete Jan Thiel Guide

Jan Thiel isn't just a location on a map – it's where the dushi life comes alive. Discover why this southeastern gem has become Curaçao's most sought-after neighborhood.

Welcome to our home! Jan Thiel is where the Tommy Coconut family has put down roots, and once you experience it, you'll understand why. It's the perfect blend of pristine beaches, world-class dining, vibrant nightlife, and authentic island community. This is your insider's guide to living the Jan Thiel lifestyle.

Why Jan Thiel is Special

BEACHES

World-Class Beaches

Jan Thiel Beach is the crown jewel – a pristine stretch of white sand with calm, crystal-clear waters perfect for families. Multiple beach clubs offer loungers, water sports, and restaurants. Just minutes away, you'll find secluded coves and snorkeling spots that feel like your private paradise.

DINING

Culinary Paradise

From beachfront seafood to upscale fusion cuisine, Jan Thiel's restaurant scene rivals any Caribbean destination. Walk to dinner at sunset, enjoy fresh catch prepared by local chefs, or grab casual beach bites – all within your neighborhood.

NATURE

Spanish Water Bay

One of the Caribbean's most beautiful natural harbors is your backyard. Spanish Water offers protected sailing, kayaking through mangroves, waterfront dining, and some of the most stunning sunset views on the island. It's a world within a world.

CONVENIENCE

Everything You Need

Supermarkets, pharmacies, dive shops, and local services are all within easy reach. You're just 15 minutes from Willemstad's historic center and the airport, yet it feels like a world away from the hustle. It's the perfect balance of convenience and tranquility.

ACTIVITIES

Adventure Central

Diving, snorkeling, kitesurfing, paddleboarding, sailing – if it involves water, you can do it from Jan Thiel. The area is also home to hiking trails with panoramic views, and the annual Kite Surfing World Cup happens right on your beach.

COMMUNITY

Authentic Island Living

Jan Thiel maintains its authentic Caribbean character while welcoming visitors warmly. Local families, expats, and regular visitors create a diverse, friendly community. You'll recognize faces at the beach and coffee shop – that's the Jan Thiel way.

Where to Eat in Jan Thiel

Zanzibar Beach

Right on Jan Thiel Beach, Zanzibar offers fresh seafood, international cuisine, and killer cocktails. The seafood platter for two is legendary, and sunset dinner here is a must-do experience.

$$ • Beachfront • Seafood & International

Piazza Boutique

Authentic Italian cuisine in a charming garden setting. Their fresh pasta is made daily, and the wood-fired pizzas are the best on the island. This is where locals celebrate special occasions.

$$$ • Italian • Romantic Garden

Karakter Beach Bar

The ultimate beach bar vibe – feet in the sand, cold beer in hand, and the best burgers in Jan Thiel. Sunday afternoons feature live music and a BBQ. It's where the community comes together.

$$ • Beach Bar • Burgers & BBQ

Boka 19

Built on stilts over Spanish Water, this casual waterfront spot serves fresh fish tacos, cold beers, and sunset views that'll take your breath away. The fish tacos on Tuesday are a local tradition.

$$ • Waterfront • Casual Seafood

Papaya Beach

One of three beach clubs on Jan Thiel Beach, Papaya offers all-day dining from breakfast through sunset. Their fresh juices and smoothie bowls are perfect after a morning swim, and their sunset cocktails are Instagram-worthy.

$$ • Beach Club • All-Day Dining

Things to Do in Jan Thiel

Water Sports

Kitesurfing, paddleboarding, kayaking, and jet skiing – all available right from the beach. Multiple water sports centers offer rentals and lessons for all skill levels.

Diving & Snorkeling

Some of Curaçao's best dive sites are minutes away by boat. Shore diving and snorkeling spots dot the coastline, with crystal-clear visibility year-round.

Hiking Trails

Explore trails offering panoramic views of Spanish Water and the Caribbean Sea. Early morning hikes reward you with stunning sunrises and the chance to spot native wildlife.

Sailing & Boat Tours

Charter a catamaran for sunset cruises, book fishing trips, or rent a boat to explore hidden coves. The marina offers full services for sailors and boat enthusiasts.

Beach Relaxation

Sometimes the best activity is doing nothing. Rent a lounger, order beachside service, and let the Caribbean work its magic. This is the true dushi life.

Nightlife

Beach bars transform into lively evening spots with live music, DJs, and dancing. It's laid-back island nightlife – casual, friendly, and always welcoming.

Insider Tips for Jan Thiel

Best Times to Visit the Beach

Arrive early (before 9 AM) for the calmest waters and best parking. Late afternoon (after 3 PM) offers gorgeous light and fewer crowds. Weekdays are generally quieter than weekends when locals come out to play.

Parking Tips

Jan Thiel Beach has paid parking ($3-5). Arrive early or use the overflow lots nearby. If you're staying at a Tommy Coconut villa, we'll show you the local parking secrets and quieter beach access points.

Shopping & Services

Van den Tweel supermarket is your go-to for groceries – just 5 minutes away. There's also a pharmacy, dive shops, and local boutiques nearby. We'll provide you with a detailed neighborhood map showing everything you need.

Local Etiquette

Say "Bon dia" (good day) to locals you pass – it goes a long way. Don't leave valuables unattended on the beach. Support local businesses, and always ask before taking photos of people. Being a good neighbor matters in this close-knit community.
